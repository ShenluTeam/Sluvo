## Style Prompt

Sluvo Community preview: a creator publishes a canvas card into a dark gold community gallery, then other creators fork it into new remix paths. The feeling is quiet creator network energy, not a social feed.

## Colors

- Background: `#050505`
- Panel: `#0d0b07`
- Gold: `#d6b56d`
- Cream: `#fff1c7`
- Soft line: `rgba(255, 241, 199, 0.22)`

## Typography

- UI labels: `"Noto Sans JP", "Segoe UI", sans-serif`
- Counts: `"Cascadia Mono", "Consolas", monospace`

## What NOT to Do

- No noisy social metrics wall.
- No bright multicolor community palette.
- No giant avatar grid.
- No text-heavy explanation.
