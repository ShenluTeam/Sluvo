## Style Prompt

Sluvo Canvas Skill preview: a node chain is compressed into a reusable skill pack, then copied into a fresh canvas. It should feel like creators are turning a repeatable workflow into an installable method.

## Colors

- Background: `#050505`
- Panel: `#0d0b07`
- Gold: `#d6b56d`
- Cream: `#fff1c7`
- Muted gold: `rgba(255, 241, 199, 0.58)`

## Typography

- UI labels: `"Noto Sans JP", "Segoe UI", sans-serif`
- Status labels: `"Cascadia Mono", "Consolas", monospace`

## What NOT to Do

- No marketplace clutter.
- No fake download UI.
- No colorful app-store palette.
- No tiny unreadable text blocks.
