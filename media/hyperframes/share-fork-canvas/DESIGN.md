## Style Prompt

Sluvo homepage open-canvas sharing preview: dark cinematic canvas, warm gold interface light, glass panels, fine node graph lines, publishing pulse, forked workflow copies. The video should feel like a product UI micro-demo rather than a marketing bumper.

## Colors

- Background: `#050505`
- Ink panel: `#0d0b07`
- Gold accent: `#d6b56d`
- Cream text: `#fff1c7`
- Muted line: `rgba(255, 241, 199, 0.24)`

## Typography

- UI and Chinese labels: `"Noto Sans JP", "Segoe UI", sans-serif`
- Numeric/status labels: `"Cascadia Mono", "Consolas", monospace`

## What NOT to Do

- No purple or blue tech gradients.
- No decorative blobs or oversized orbs.
- No busy tiny paragraphs; the embed will be small inside the homepage card.
- No bright white full-screen flashes.
