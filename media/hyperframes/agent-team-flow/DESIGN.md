## Style Prompt

Sluvo Agent Team preview: a dark gold canvas where director, writer, storyboard, art, and render agents are routed as a staged creative team. The viewer should see planning become execution through connected task cards and status pulses.

## Colors

- Background: `#050505`
- Panel: `#0d0b07`
- Gold: `#d6b56d`
- Cream: `#fff1c7`
- Success gold: `#f2d58b`

## Typography

- UI labels: `"Noto Sans JP", "Segoe UI", sans-serif`
- Status labels: `"Cascadia Mono", "Consolas", monospace`

## What NOT to Do

- No provider-debug language.
- No busy tables; this must read inside a small homepage card.
- No purple/blue AI gradients.
- No decorative blobs.
